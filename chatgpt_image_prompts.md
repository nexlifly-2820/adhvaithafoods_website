# ChatGPT Image Generation Guide

Since ChatGPT (DALL-E) can only generate 1 or 2 images per message, it's best to give it a "Master Prompt" first to set the rules, and then feed it the products in small batches.

## Step 1: Copy and paste this Master Prompt into ChatGPT
```text
Act as a professional food photographer. I need you to generate highly realistic product images for my Indian food e-commerce store using DALL-E. 

For EVERY image I ask for, you must follow this exact style and constraint:
"A highly realistic and authentic close-up food photography shot of [PRODUCT NAME], served fully inside a beautiful traditional Indian bowl. It must look highly appetizing with rich textures, placed in a rustic traditional setting with clean bright lighting. Ultra high quality real photo."

Do not use illustrations or cartoons—only hyper-realistic photography. Do you understand?
```
